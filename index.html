dylan's compilation is a collection of my miscellaneous notes, pictures, essays, ideas, music, and other creations. I've curated them from various notebooks and hard drives.

This collection serves as an extension to my personal "commonplace book," which is a real, much messier book.

The compilation is "sorted" chronologically, effectively how the commonplace book is written. Entries are displayed as a timeline to show progression of my "stuff."

Some information may be redacted for privacy.
